Delicious Miso Soup

Description

You can't have a sushi dinner and not include miso soup i mean c'mon people.

Ingredients

  • Seaweed
  • Tofu
  • Miso paste
  • Water
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Throw everything you've gathered into a medium sized pot
  2. Set the heat on your stovetop to medium
  3. Wait about ten minutes or so
  4. Stir the pot
  5. Enjoy!
