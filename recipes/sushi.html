Tasty Salmon Sushi Roll

Description

We're going to make a fun lil salmon maki roll using some common ingredients you can buy from your local H-Mart! All you'll need are a few ingredients, some patience, and a love for raw fish.

Ingredients

  • Seaweed
  • Sushi-grade salmon
  • Sushi rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi

Steps

  1. Lay out all the ingredients on the kitchen counter
  2. Lay seaweed flat on surface and place 3 inches of rice on seaweed
  3. Place salmon on top of seaweed
  4. Top that bad boy off with a lil wasabi
  5. Roll up your sushi and dip it in soy sauce before consuming
  6. Profit!
