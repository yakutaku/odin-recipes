Yummy Tuna Handroll

Description

It's tuna time, my friends. Let's get it.

Ingredients

  • Seaweed
  • Sushi-grade tuna
  • Sushi rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Lay out all the ingredients on the kitchen counter
  2. Lay seaweed flat on surface and place 3 inches of rice on seaweed
  3. Place tuna on top of seaweed
  4. Roll that lil puppy up
  5. Top with freshly chopped scallions
  6. Return to this website and offer your gratitude for this incredible recipe
